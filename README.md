# Data-Driven-Amazon-Product-Research-and-Validation
Conducted data-driven product research and validation using market data, keyword demand, competitor analysis, and revenue trends. Evaluated metrics such as search volume, competition, pricing, and sales potential to identify profitable product opportunities and support evidence-based product launch decisions.
Calculated estimated profit margin of the product using all the Amazon feeses like FBA and referral fee, Advertising costs and sourcing costs

• Amazon Product Data
• Excel / Google Sheets
• Data Visualization
